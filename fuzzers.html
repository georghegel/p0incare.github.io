Fuzzing the Lab: From Dumb Mutations to Smart Coverage

By The Fuzzing Desk — June 12, 2025

Fuzzers remain essential to uncovering logic flaws and memory corruption bugs. This article contrasts mutation-based fuzzers with grammar-aware and coverage-guided tools, and offers guidance on improving results in constrained testbeds.

Key takeaways: seed quality matters; instrumentation increases find rates; and prioritizing test cases by crash uniqueness reduces triage effort. The story includes a simple harness example for a network service and a primer on crash deduplication.
