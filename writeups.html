CTF Post-Mortem: A Moderately Tricky Buffer Challenge

By Capture Crew — April 2, 2025

This writeup reconstructs a CTF challenge that required a stack pivot and crafting a ROP chain to bypass mitigations. Step-by-step, we describe the exploit development process from the initial reconnaissance to final exploit stabilization.

Included are annotated disassembly snippets, debugging notes, and the thought process behind each pivot. This serves both as a practical guide and as a record of the reasoning behind exploit choices.

Readers are reminded to apply lessons ethically and within permitted scopes.

Final notes summarize debugging heuristics that saved hours: watch for off-by-one writes, verify stack alignment, and prefer reproducible, minimalistic payloads.
